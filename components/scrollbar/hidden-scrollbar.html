Scroll bar container

A hidden scroll bar can be used to reduce visual clutter on the UI. For example, you can use a hidden scroll bar for a tree view component.

  • When the cursor hovers over a scrollable area, display the slider grip to indicate that there is more content to display.
  • When the cursor hovers over the vertical area under the slider grip, display a translucent background to indicate the slider track.
  • When the cursor hovers over the slider grip, change the slider grip color.
