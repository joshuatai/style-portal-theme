Popover help should be a bottom-right popover style. Please refer to Popovers component.

Popover help under input fields

A form can be difficult to read if it contains too much text. Use popovers that only appear when the corresponding fields are focused to reduce the amount of text.

At least 8 characters consisting of :
  • Alphanumeric characters (A-Z, a-z, 0-9) with both upper and lower case letters and number characters.
  • At least one special character, e.g. ` ~ ! @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) - _ + = [ ]{ } \ |< > , . / ? : ; ' "
